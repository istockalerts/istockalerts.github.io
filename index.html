Welcome To iStockAlerts

Bridging the gap between institutional firms and the everyday investor by blending together mobile, financial, and social arenas.

Looks Great, Works Better

iStockAlerts is an easy-to-use mobile application designed to notify the average investor on when to execute trades. Our proprietary technology makes educated stock picks by identifying trends and providing users with adequate tools needed for trading. Our goal is to unite the trading community and provide them with the tools they need to succeed. Our automated market advice is a unique blend of financial services and mobile technology, and it is truly the next step in creating a community of more educated investors.

Our Team



Eli Engelman

Eli is 19 years-old and lives in the Northern Suburbs of Chicago. He is interested in business, marketing, and finance. After working in an Investment Bank in New York City during the summer of 2013, Eli noticed an information gap between institutional investors and the everyday trader. Eli founded iStockAlerts, LLC in the fall of 2013 and set out to make an application designed to help the everyday trader make wiser financial decisions. Eli is a die-hard Cubs fan and avid tennis player. He began attending Tulane University in the fall of 2014 and is a proud member of the Zeta Beta Tau Fraternity.

Joe Rabil
